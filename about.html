About Us

Established in 2023 - Walled Lake Community Church has been called into being by God and exists for His glory. Our church is composed of forgiven individuals who, through Christ, have become the people of the triune God. The Creator God is our Father, Jesus Christ is our Lord and Head of the Church, and the Holy Spirit is our life and power.

We are a member of the Missionary Church https://mcusa.org/

Our mailing address is P.O. Box 517, Walled Lake, 48390
